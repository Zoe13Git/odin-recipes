Ingredients

  • 3-3½ pound (1-1.5kg) chicken
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 4-5 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoon (12g) ginger, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon (6-7g) paprika(grined pepper)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon (10g) Maggi or bouillon powder
  • 2 green onion, chopped (whites and green parts)
  • 3-5 tablespoons (5-9g) parsley, basil, or other fresh herb
  • 1-3 scotch bonnet or habanero peppers
  • salt and white pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup (60ml) njansa

Steps

  1. Puree the spices in the blender or food processor, pulsing until everything is finely ground.
  2. Add the chicken and spices to a large pot with 7-8 cups of water. Bring to a boil and simmer until the chicken is tender.
  3. Discard bay leaf, then adjust for seasonings and thickness (add more water if desired)
  4. Serve Hot
