Ndole

Description

Ndolé is a flavorful Cameroonian dish featuring stewed peanuts and bitter leaves. It’s a national treasure on Cameroonian plates, celebrating the union of rich flavors.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups washed bitter leaves
  • 3 cups ground peanuts
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 piece ginger
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 1 Leek/spring onion
  • 1/2 cup ground crayfish
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1/2 kg beef
  • Salt and cube
  • Pepper
  • Shrimps/prawns to garnish
  • Sweet potatoes or ripe plantain

Steps

  1. Soak the peanuts in water for about 1 hour and blend afterwards. Alternatively you can parboil the peanuts for about 5 mins.
  2. Blend the ginger, garlic, 1 onion, leeks, white pepper
  3. Boil the meat with half onion, 3 cups of water, pinch of salt and a cube (maggi) for about 20 mins until tender
  4. Warm 1/4 cup of oil in a pot. Put in 1/4 chopped onions and fry for about 30 secs.
  5. Pour in the peanuts and add 3 cups of water. Cook for about 15 mins. stir from time to time.
  6. Add to it the ginger, garlic, white pepper and leek mixture. stir properly
  7. Add the ndole (vegetables) in small quantities while stiring. Add the meat with stock, crayfish, hot pepper, cube and salt. Allow to simmer for 10-15 mins till thick.
  8. Add the ndole (vegetables) in small quantities while stiring. Add the meat with stock, crayfish, hot pepper, cube and salt. Allow to simmer for 10-15 mins till thick.
  9. Peel your sweet potatoes or ripe plantain and boil for 20 mins. You can boil with the peelings and peel afterwards.
  10. Serve your ndole hot with red wine. Bonne appetite.
